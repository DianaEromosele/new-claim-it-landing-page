What is Claim it!

A product giveaway platform. More specifically Claim it! is a weekly sweepstakes of awesome free stuff people can claim from their smartphones.

  • FOR YOU

    Only one app gives you the chance to win amazing free products from awesome brands, every week.

  • FOR ADVERTISERS

    Our largely millennial audience gladly watch short video ads for the chance to claim amazing free offers. We sell views not impressions.

  • FOR BRANDS

    Product giveaways are grouped into weekly campaigns and treated as the featured content .

  • FOR RETAILERS

    Our proximity based special offers drive potential customers directly to your store for the chance to win exciting instant prizes.

How does it Work

Claim it! offers the chance to win amazing free products in a specific city or town. Users put in a claim on that week’s offers and wait for the Sunday drawing. Winners have one week to pick up their prizes.
